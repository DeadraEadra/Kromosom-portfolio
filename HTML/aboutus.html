OUR STORY

How Donald Trump made
Andrea visit the cafe

While casually walking down the street, Andrea and her dad, Lars, were unexpectedly invited by Thomas, the previous owner, to see… his toilet with Donald Trump!

They couldn’t say “no” to such an offer!

From the manager to...
the owner?!

Soon enough, Andrea started working in the cafe. Some time later, she became the manager. Unfortunately, after some time Thomas was forced to give up the place, however, he made Andrea an offer - it was then, when she bought the cafe.

On the
queer mission

While always wanting to do something for the queer community in Aarhus, Andrea didn't have any specific plan for that. Becoming an owner of a cafe has changed it.

"KROMOSOM"

We all have chromosomes in our DNA. Each of them hold specific ‘instructions’ that make us unique. This is why our aim is to appreciate every single chromosome (dk: “kromosom”) that makes us who and how we are.

This is us now

OUR MISSION

We are the management, consisting of Andrea as the over-all manager and lucky owner, Soni as the day-to-day manager and trusty janitor, Katrine (often referred to by their nickname Længste - or Long one) as the finance manager and treasurer, Lars as the bar- and purchasing manager, and last but definitely not least, Dicte as the PR- and SoMe manager.

All of us from the whole team of Kromosom, are very much looking forward to welcoming you!

SYMBOLS

At Kromosom we cherish individuality, therefore, we have many inside jokes and elements that have a deeper meaning to us. Want to feel more like a part of our community? We encourage you to learn more about them!

Person

Person

whoever you are

No matter what your size, age, colour or gender is - you are a person and we value you. Therefore, the Person is a really important part of our logo

We want to tell everyone that they matter for us, regardless of physical appearance and information written in their ID.

Bench

Bench

colorful social spot

If the world was perfect, absolutely everyone would own a rainbow bench! Unfortunately, it isn’t but don’t you worry - we have one!

There is no better place for a talk about life, smoking session or Instagram photo - everything in one. Additionally, let’s admit it - it is so beautifully colourful!

Barbie

Barbie

toxic and iconic

For sure you know Barbies! The dolls that almost everyone has played with, at least once. Incredibly famous, yet incredibly unhealthy and toxic - especially when it comes to body image they used to represent.

We want to raise awareness in terms of body shaming and say loudly “no” to everyone that criticises people based on their physical appearance. In Kromosom we respect you however you look.
